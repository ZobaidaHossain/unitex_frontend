    Why With Us

    Quality Control

    Quality means customer needs are to be satisfied. From the first investigation to find what the potential customer for a new product really wants, through design, specification, controlled manufacture, and sale. There are a number of factors on which the quality fitness of the garments industry is based such as performance, reliability, durability, visual and perceived quality of the garment. Quality needs to be defined in terms of a particular framework of cost. Unitex provides high quality control of their products.

    Sustainibility

    At present Bangladesh is the third largest garment manufacturer and exporter country in the world . Even though the garment manufacturing industries have become the backbone of the country's economic growth and social safety, it also possesses some unexpected threats recently. There are a number of fire accidents, which caused death of hundreds of garment workers and fires have become common issue in present situation.Unitex provide all time power,air,fire safety for their workers.Our floor are fully coated with floar epoxy.we have fire hydrant,fire door for safety issue.

    Green Technology

    Bangladesh Garment Manufacturers and Exporters Association (BGMEA) in association with USGBC and GBCI launched “LEED Green Factory Award” in recognition and celebration of Bangladesh’s RMG sector’s strides in green industrialization.Bangladesh’s RMG sector now has 67 Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design (LEED) green factories.Unitex follow this green technology rules and increase power and energy and use LED lights all over the factory.

    Health & Safety

    Our workers strictly followed the term health and safety.They use mask, gloves during work.they use head headphone to save their ear from noise pollution like machines sound.We have fire hydrant and fire protection door for fire safety and air compressure for air safety.

    Code of Conduct

    Social accountability standards have been developed by the international organizations such as Fair Labor Association.Worldwide responsible apparel production ,Council on economic priority accredition.Reputated brand buyers in large supply chain have taken the guideline from those organizations anf formulated their own standard of code of conduct and also the acceptance criteria.The basic principles of code of conduct have been derived from the principle of international human rights.Unitex lbel Ltd folow all those rules of code of conduct.

    Workers Benifit

    worker benefits and benefits in kind include various types of non-wage compensation provided to employes in addition to their normal wages or salaries .In instances where an employee exchanges wages for some other form of benefit is generally referred to as a salary packaging or 'salary exchange' arrangement. In most countries, most kinds of employee benefits are taxable to at least some degree. Unitex ensure their workers safety and their accurate salary and leave.
